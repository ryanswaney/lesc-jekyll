As Climate Change, Agriculture and Food Security (CCAFS) Head of Research, Sonja Vermeulen helps the team to produce research outputs that synthesise across the flagships and regions, and to engage with policy processes and partners, particularly at the global level. Prior to her position at CCAFS, Dr Vermeulen served as Director of the Programme on Business and Sustainable Development at the International Institute for Environment and Development (IIED). Earlier in her career she worked in research with the University of Zimbabwe, the World Wide Fund for Nature and the Center for International Forestry Research.
